== Feature list for Route Finder ==

1. Store complete network of lines for Objectville.
2. Store stations along the lines.
3. It should be able to take a starting station and a destination 
   station and figure out a route to travel.
4. Should be able to print a report of the route chosen, including 
   which lines to take, which stations are passed on a line and 
   details of when a passenger needs to get off one line and get on
   another line.
5. Might also consider store sightseeing places per station.
